Mega Quiz 2017


PREFACE


Infocus - a social organization,  a Mohanpur based social organization  has been organizing a mega quiz competition  for last two consecutive years. Job aspirants, quiz lovers, students of schools, colleges, universities,  from entire sub-division and areas adjacent to it take part in this quiz competition with huge enthusiasm. The event become huge popular and turned into a festival of knowledge in our area. Now 3rd edition of this quiz competition will be organized on 3rd September, 2017 (Sunday) at Sanskritik Bhawan, Mohanpur.


GENERAL INFORMATION


  • Infocus - a social organization,  a Mohanpur based social organization  has been organizing a mega quiz competition  for last two consecutive years.
  • 2(two) persons are required to form each team.
  • There is no age limitation of the participants.
  • Participating team must pay Rs. 30 as Registration Fee at the time of registration.
  • Registration starts from 1st August 2017, & it will be closed on 30th August, 2017
  • A registration number will be issued to each registered team.
  • Participating teams shall have to bring a BLACK BALL POINT PEN and the registration token only inside the exam hall during the screening test.
  • All the teams have to go through a screening test. Screening test will be held on 3rd Sep, 2017 at 10:00am at Sansktritik Bhawan, Airan Chowmohani, Mohanpur.
  • Participating teams shall have to report at 9:00am for appearing in the screening test at Sansktritik Bhawan, Airan Chowmohani, Mohanpur.
  • Infocus Mega quiz-2017 will be inaugurated at 11:00 am on the said date, in presence of eminent personalities of Tripura.
  • Main Quiz Competition will be started from 12O’clock.
  • Light refreshment will be provided to each participating team.
  • Use of Mobile phone or any electronic gadgets are not allowed inside the hall room.
  • Infocus will not be liable for the belongings of participants/audience during the entire quiz program.
  • Questions in the quiz competitions will be asked from the following topics: Tripura, Disaster Management & Environment, Indian Polity, Indian History, Indian Geography, Science & technology, Sports, Cinema, Mythology, Computer, Current affairs, Contemporary World History, Music, Information Technology, Digital India etc.


RULES AND REGULATION OF QUIZ CONTEST


SCREENING TEST


  • All the teams have to go through a screening test.
  • The screening test will consist of 20 MCQs, each carrying 1 mark.
  • Each questions shall have 4(four) alternatives.
  • Questions must be answered on the Question Sheet by putting a tick mark in the appropriate option like A, B, C, D.
  • There will be NEGATIVE marking for any wrong answer. For each wrong answer 0.5 Mark will be deducted.
  • Each of the participating team will write their registration number at the specified space on the sheet.
  • Total time allotted for answering is 10 minute.
  • The questions for this test will be asked from Current affairs & General Knowledge.
  • In the case of TIE while selecting twelve teams for preliminary round, another special screening test will be conducted among the teams having equal score.
  • No one should be allowed to go outside of room during screening test.


PRELIMINARY SEGMENT


  • On the basis of Screening Test result, best twelve teams will be selected for the preliminary segment.
  • There will be two sessions for the preliminary segment. Out of twelve selected teams, randomly selected six teams will be allowed to participate in each session.
  • Based on the score of preliminary round best six teams will be selected for final segment.
  • There will be three rounds in each session of preliminary segment.
  • On all the controversial issues/ in case of TIE (if any) and in regard to correctness or authenticity of answers provided, decision of the quiz master shall be final.


FINAL SEGMENT


  •  In the final segment there will be six rounds.


AUDIENCE SEGMENT


  •  Unanswered questions shall be asked to the audience. In addition there will be different segments on various issues, topics, events etc. for the audience.  The sponsors may be involved in the audience round.  


PRIZE


  •  Best three teams will be awarded with First, Second and Third prize respectively and rest nine teams selected in the preliminary round shall be awarded with special prizes.
  •  There will be attractive prizes for the audience who come with the correct answer in the audience round.


Note! Infocus -A Social Organization preserves all the rights to change/modify the rules and regulations stated above and may take any decision under special circumstances.
